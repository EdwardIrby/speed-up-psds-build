## Figma

At Pluralsight we use Figma to work collaboratively and openly on design. All teams in our Figma Organization should be open and accessible to anyone in the company to view.

The following libraries of reusable components are accessible to internal users:

### Core libraries

- [PSDS - Core](https://www.figma.com/file/S9QHDvzv20ut7ICalO8cFV/PSDS-Core): Colors
- [PSDS - Web](https://www.figma.com/file/YsTklBecdddy9RZ3HXddIseD/PSDS-Web): Web components
- [PSDS - Icons](https://www.figma.com/file/HVFUT0XlrkfsTMde6PMWLITj/PSDS-Icons): 24x24 icons

### Helper libraries

- [Imagery](https://www.figma.com/file/K575lLx48kMoPQGDlbdiWXv7/%CE%9E-PSDS-Imagery): Tech icons, avatars, sample content imagery
- [Brand](https://www.figma.com/file/DYZV1wR89TxDo63lEngOLvwm/%CE%9E-PSDS-Brand): Company & product logos
- [Device Mockups](https://www.figma.com/file/Pzs0dRGr79QgUI6QbKbbG6zu/%CE%9E-Device-Mockups): Phones, tablets, computers, displays and TVs.
- [Extras](https://www.figma.com/file/U7JwbMLo37WPhu3StSq86Kyt/%CE%9E-PSDS-Extras): Browser chrome, onscreen keyboards, etc.
- [Figma Thumbnails](https://www.figma.com/file/ERFURfSVuAuyxVEEP2VA8u/%CE%9E-Figma-Thumbnails): Cover thumbnail templates for your Figma files

## Design system meetup
